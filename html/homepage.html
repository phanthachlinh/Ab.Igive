START YOUR CAMPAIGN

Helping refugees has never been so easy

You can do anything you want to raise money for refugees. You can pledge your next birthday, run a marathon, throw a party or even dye your hairs in blue if you want to. And it only takes two minutes to get involved. See how it works.

STEP #1

See that 70.8 million people were forcibly displaced last year, and decide to help.

It only takes two minutes to set up a campaign. Decide what to do. Pick a name. Pick a photo. And just like that, you’ll be ready to start raising money to help build a world where every person forced to flee can build a better future.

STEP #2

Spread the word about your campaign and ask your friends to give.

Once your campaign is set up, tell everyone about it. The more people you tell, the quicker you’ll reach your goal. Then sit back and watch the donations to roll in. Do not forget to say thank you on behalf of all refugees you will help!

STEP #3

See how we use the money you raised and tell the good news to your friends.

For over half a century, UNHCR has helped millions of people to restart their lives. They include refugees, returnees, stateless people, the internally displaced and asylum-seekers. Our protection, shelter, health and education has been crucial, healing broken pasts and building brighter futures. You can be proud.
